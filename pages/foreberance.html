Section 6: No Payments while Under Review

By submitting this application, you may request to have all your federal loans placed into forbearance and to stop collections on any federal loans currently in default.

Please note that interest will continue to accumulate on these loans and will be added to the amount you still owe. If your application is denied or partially approved, the amount you owe may be higher.

You do not have to place your loans in Forbearance or Stopped collections to apply for borrower defense relief. Please read the question and answers section carefully.
