Section 4: Basis for Borrower Defense

Please note that you only need to provide information for the sections below that apply to you, but you must complete at least one section.

Promises of Employment
Including promises of future employment, likelihood of finding a job, eligibility for certification or licensure in your field of study, how many students graduate, or earnings after graduation.
Transferring Credits
Including transferring your credits from this school to other schools.
School Costs
Including how much your classes would cost, how you would pay for your education of loan repayment, or other issues about the cost of your education
Career Services
Including the availability or quality of job placement, career services, or the school's connections to the employers within your field of study.
Educational Services
Including availability or internships, qualifications of teachers, instructional methods, or other types of educations services.
Admissions of Urgency to Enroll
Including the importance of enrolling immediately, the consequences of not enrolling, how difficult it was to be admitted, or anything else about the admission process.
Such as: Your school failing to perform its obligations under its contract with you, there is a judgment against your school in a Federal court, a State court, or in front of an administrative board? Is there some other reason you feel your school misled you?
Likelihood of finding a job, how many students graduate, and/or earnings after graduation?
